About Me

I'm Geraldo Nascimento, software engineer and game developer.

My work has been featured on Kotaku, Killscreen, Siliconera, The Guardian and other gaming outlets.

I'm immensely passionate about the development of video games, having created an action game from scratch, gathered a team, achieved Steam Greenlight status and organised a Kickstarter.

I'm interested in sustainable game making and excellent software development practices. Software and game creation techniques fascinate me.

This is where I share my creative process, where I fill the cracks between the games I make and the software I write. Live from the intersection of software and creativity.
